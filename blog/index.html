  • Widespread Misconceptions about U.S. Military Salaries

    November 28th, 2014

    I've noticed that very few people actually know how much money military members earn. Even among military members, there is a surprising amount of misinformation exchanged and a general lack of knowledge on the subject. While a quick google search will indeed yield results, the numbers shown on most websites do not tell the whole story. Aside from being formatted in tables that are unintelligible to the average person, Basic Pay figures are not comprehensive. There are sizable monetary allowances not mentioned on those pay tables and significant tax breaks that need to be taken into account before arriving at the final equivalent civilian salary. Many people would be surprised at what those final numbers turn out to be.

    Let me clear things up and also explain why I think overcoming these misconceptions is a very important factor in attracting and retaining quality military members.
